About

I'm Owen Li, an engineer at the intersection of mechanical systems and aerospace design. I'm drawn to problems where precision matters — where a millimeter or a millisecond changes everything.

My work spans structural analysis, fluid dynamics, and systems engineering. I love the moment when a concept sketch becomes something that actually works in the real world.

Outside of engineering, you'll find me building things by hand, reading about the history of aviation, or somewhere with good mountains.
