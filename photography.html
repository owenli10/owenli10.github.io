About

Photography is where I go when the equations stop. I'm drawn to industrial texture, long exposures, and the structural honesty of things built to last — whether that's a suspension bridge or a weathered doorframe.

I shoot primarily on 35mm film, with a preference for grain over polish. The imperfection is the point. Engineering taught me to eliminate failure; photography taught me to find beauty in it.

Based in the United States, shooting wherever the light is interesting.
